About

Harsh Maha Vidhalaya

Harsh Maha Vidyalaya is an esteemed institution affiliated with Raja Mahendra Pratap Singh State University, Aligarh. We are committed to providing exceptional higher education and fostering intellectual growth among our students.

Located on Mahrara Road, Sahpau, Hathras, our campus offers a serene and conducive environment for learning and personal development. At Harsh Maha Vidyalaya, we believe in empowering students through quality education and holistic experiences.

We currently offer two undergraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Arts (BA) and Bachelor of Science (BSc). These courses are designed to provide a strong foundation in their respective disciplines while nurturing critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills. Our dedicated faculty members, who are renowned experts in their fields, are committed to guiding and mentoring students on their educational journey.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, sports facilities, and dedicated spaces for artistic expression. These resources create an ideal environment for students to explore their academic interests, engage in research, and develop a well-rounded personality.

Join our esteemed academic community at Harsh Maha Vidyalaya, located on Mahrara Road, Sahpau, Hathras. Discover your potential, unlock endless possibilities, and prepare for a future filled with success and fulfillment.
